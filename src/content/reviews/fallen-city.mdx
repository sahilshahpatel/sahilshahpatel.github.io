I am now months past having read this while I write this review, so I'll keep it short and sweet. Fallen City was an entertaining version of that YA dystopian adventure that you know and love, but with more adult romance than say The Hunger Games. The ideas and lore behind the book were also very interesting, but since the second book isn't out yet it's hard to really judge that. Overall I enjoyed it and would recommend it to fans of the genre, though personally it did not hit as hard as Adrienne Young's more adult fiction.

- Maas is fine at romance but bad at fantasy. As the most fantasy and least romance in the trilogy, this book was the worst for me
- Maas' main weakness is her world building and general high-level story coherence. It feels like she decided the path of her story as she wrote it instead of having a plan
- List of stupid conveniences and world building gaffs...
- Writing ticks:
    - Once, twice
    - e.g. Color and light and shadow
    - Using "that/those" where "the" would suffice

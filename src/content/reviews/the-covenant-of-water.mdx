The Covenant of Water is a very long book, but with good reason. It takes you through the story of a family over generations (in a way similar to Isabel Allende's The House of Spirits). The story gets very hard to read at times as characters just act in ways that are so frustrating and so harmful towards others. But it paints a very beautiful picture of Kerala through the dramatic changes of the 20th century. Ultimately the story was quite good, and I was happy to have read it afterwards even though it did feel like an ordeal in the middle there.
